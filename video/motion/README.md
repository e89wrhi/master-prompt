# 🌀 Motion & Generative Prompts

Prompts designed directly for After Effects ideation, or for driving Video-to-Video and Text-to-Video models (RunwayML, Sora).

## Available Categories

- [**After Effects Expressions**](./after_effects_expressions/index.md): Utilizing AI to write complex JavaScript expressions for wiggle, loopOut, and specific physics.
- [**Kinetic Typography Rules**](./kinetic_typography_rules/index.md): Guidelines for fast, impactful on-screen text animations.
- [**RunwayML Generation Prompts**](./runwayml_generation_prompts/index.md): Camera motion variables and seed consistency for gen-video.
- [**Sora Physics Prompts**](./sora_physics_prompts/index.md): Complex physical constraints (e.g. fluid dynamics, gravity shifts) for text-to-video.
