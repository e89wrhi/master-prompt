# ✂️ Editing & Post-Production Prompts

Guidelines for structuring edits, color theory, and audio leveling directives for human editors or AI copilots.

## Available Categories

- [**Audio Mixing Stems**](./audio_mixing_stems/index.md): EQ curves, compression rules, and balancing dialogue vs B-Roll.
- [**Color Grading LUTs**](./color_grading_luts/index.md): Directives for achieving specific cinematic looks (e.g. Teal & Orange, Bleach Bypass).
- [**Pacing and Cut Guidelines**](./pacing_and_cut_guidelines/index.md): Rules for J-cuts, L-cuts, and retaining viewer retention.
- [**Premiere Pro Transitions**](./premiere_pro_transitions/index.md): Directing specific physical or digital wipes and blurs.
